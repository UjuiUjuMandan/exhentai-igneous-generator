1. Privacy Policy
1.1. What I know about you

1. Your IP address
2. Your country based on IP address
3. Your web browser's HTTP header including User-Agent, which includes your operating system, web browser, and device type (mobile or desktop).
4. Your E-Hentai account credentials: ipb_member_id and ipb_pass_hash, granting full access to your account.

1.2. How will your data be logged

The default backend services each run under that platform's own default logging configuration. These defaults (for example, AWS CloudWatch) may record request metadata such as your IP address, and possibly the request payload - which could include the sensitive credentials above. I haven't audited exactly what each platform's defaults capture.

Cloudflare Web Analytics will log your IP address, country and User-Agent information.

1.3. How will I use your data

The web application will forward your E-Hentai account credentials to the chosen cloud provider, which then requests the E-Hentai / ExHentai website to check possible account suspension and retrieve the igneous cookie.

Cloudflare Web Analytics analyzes your data in aggregate, for overall traffic statistics.

1.4. How will I not use your data

Hacking into your E-Hentai account or selling your E-Hentai account credentials.

Sharing your E-Hentai account credentials with any party other than the chosen cloud platform.
